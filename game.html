TEST YOUR LUCK

Embarked on a thrilling journey to develop "Test Your Luck," an engaging single-player game that brings users face-to-face with the ultimate challenge against the computer. Leveraging HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the game offers two exciting options: Rock, Paper, Scissors, and Heads or Tails. Users can create accounts for an immersive experience, with local storage recording their highest scores. The project showcases a seamless fusion of web development technologies, delivering an interactive gaming platform.

ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

Within the realm of Rock, Paper, Scissors, I implemented a local storage-based leaderboard system to record and display the highest scores achieved by players. JavaScript handles the logic behind score tracking, ensuring that each victorious move is appropriately rewarded and stored. This leaderboard not only adds a competitive edge to the game but also fosters a sense of achievement among players

HEADS OR TAILS

In the Heads or Tails game module, I seamlessly integrated local storage mechanisms to record and present users' highest scores. The JavaScript implementation ensures that every flip of the virtual coin contributes to the overall score, providing players with a tangible measure of their success. Through a combination of HTML and CSS, the scoring system enhances the visual appeal of the game, offering a clear representation of each player's performance

AUTO-PLAY FUCNTION

Immerse yourself effortlessly in "Test Your Luck" with the autoplay feature. In Rock, Paper, Scissors, witness strategic battles as the computer engages in dynamic moves without user input. The Heads or Tails module adds excitement with automated coin flips, injecting an element of chance. Crafted with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the autoplay function seamlessly integrates into both games, offering players a hands-free, yet thrilling, gaming experience
